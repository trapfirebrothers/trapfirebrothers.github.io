about

Trapfire Brothers is a collaboration between Justin Garber(Guitar/Vocals) and Joe Tamburro(Percussion) based in Greenville, SC. While Justin and Joe are not of relation, there is a shared love and appreciation for listening to, sharing, and playing a wide variety of good music. Aaron Bowen fills the sound out on the keyboards. When Aaron isn't behind the boards Preston Smith or Thomas McPartland hold down the low end.

Justin Garber

Guitar & Vocals

Birthplace: Greer, SC

Favorite artists: Grateful Dead, Black Crowes, Tedeschi Trucks Band, John Scofield, The Wood Brothers, etc.

Fact: Justin twilights as an avid Brazilian Jiu Jitsu practitioner.

Joe Tamburro

Percussion

Birthplace: Los Angeles, CA

Favorite artists: Led Zeppelin, Widespread Panic, Jason Isbell, Red Hot Chili Peppers, Grateful Dead, Talking Heads, etc.

Fact: Joe's self-made bucket drumset continues to amaze sound engineers with its surprisingly awesome sound. He owns a real acoustic drum set, too.
